BASKETBALL RULES

    Schools must pay a registration fee of Rp. 400.000 to be recognized as participants in the SCB Cup Basketball Tournament, along with an additional walkout fee of Rp. 100.000.

    There are two categories of tournaments;

        Middle School - Mixed Gender

        High School - Mixed Gender

        Schools must submit a team consisting of at least 7 players and a maximum of 13 players, with only 1 coach and 1 accompanying teacher.
      Photocopies of each player's Student Card/Kartu Pelajar must be submitted to the committee.

      Each player must be registered at the school that is participating, and be aged not more than 18 years old.

      There will be five (5) players on the court at any given time.

      The matches will directly proceed to a knockout stage, with teams drawn during the technical meeting.

      The games will be played with 4 quarters, with each quarter’s duration being 10 minutes, with a total game time of 40 minutes. In between each quarter will be a 1-minute break.

      Each team must wear matching uniforms for all players, except the goalkeeper.
      In the event that a team does not have uniforms, the committee will provide bibs.

      All rules from FIBA will be implemented and enforced by referees from Perbasi.

      The final match will use stop time.

      The First, Second, and third-place finishers, along with the MVP, will be awarded a trophy along with a cash prize.

• Sekolah harus membayar biaya pendaftaran sebesar Rp. 400.000 agar diakui sebagai peserta dalam turnamen Bola Basket SCB CUP, serta tambahan biaya walkout sebesar Rp 100.000.
• Akan ada tiga kategori turnamen;

¤ Bola Basket SMP
¤ Bola Basket SMA

• Sekolah wajib menyerahkan tim berisi minimal 8 pemain dan maksimal 10 pemain dengan 1 pelatih dan 1 guru pendamping, fotokopi Kartu Pelajar wajib diserahkan.
• Semua pemain harus terdaftar di sekolah yang berpartisipasi dan berusia maksimal 18 tahun.
• Pertandingan akan langsung menuju babak sistem gugur, dengan tim diundi pada pertemuan teknis.
• Permainan akan dimainkan dengan empat kuarter, masing-masing berdurasi 10 menit, sehingga total waktu permainan adalah 40 menit. Terdapat jeda istirahat selama 5 menit di antara setiap kuarter.
• Akan ada 5 pemain dari setiap tim di lapangan pada waktu tertentu.
• Semua peraturan FIBA diterapkan dan ditegakkan oleh wasit dari Perbasi.
• Pertandingan final akan menggunakan waktu bersih.
• Pemenang Pertama, Kedua dan Ketiga akan diberi piala serta hadiah uang tunai, begitu juga dengan MVP untuk kedua kategori putra dan putri.
